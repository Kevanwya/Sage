Welcome to Sage

Sage is a web-based platform designed to support students with their academics.
Sage aims to create a centralized hub for educational resources, fostering accessibility and improving the learning experience for Secondary school students in St. Lucia.

What We Offer

Question and Answer Forum

Post academic questions and get answers from students and tutors. Browse existing discussions or contribute your own insights.

Ask a question

Submit detailed questions with optional file attachments. Get clear, accurate responses from knowledgeable users.

Tutoring Sessions

Book one-on-one tutoring sessions with experienced tutors. Schedule help for specific subjects at your convenience.
